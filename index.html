Discover the Surreal World of The Heartsnatcher

Adapted from Boris Vian’s daring and macabre masterpiece, The Heartsnatcher (L’Arrache-cœur), this cinematic experience takes you into a world where absurdity meets profound human emotions. The film explores themes of love, loss, and the intricate complexity of relationships—infused with Vian’s signature dark humor and poetic style.

At its core, The Heartsnatcher delves into the fragility of the human heart, both literally and metaphorically. Through its dreamlike visuals and compelling narrative, it invites you to question societal norms, confront personal fears, and embrace the madness of life itself.

This is not just a movie; it’s an invitation to step into a world that’s as thought-provoking as it is visually stunning.
